Two-room Flexi Flats on 99-Year Leases
(1) First-timer applicants under the Single Singapore Citizen (SSC) Scheme and Non-Citizen Spouse (NCS) Scheme, with average gross monthly income of not more than $6,000, need to pay $15,000 in additional to the flat price.
(2) First-timer Singapore citizen/Singapore permanent resident (SC/SPR) households need to pay $10,000 premium in addition to the flat price. An SC/SPR household is one where the applicant is an SC and the other family members listed in the flat application are SPRs.
Two-room Flexi Flats on 15- to 45-Year Leases (for offer only to elderly aged 55 and above)
(3) First-timer applicants under the SSC, NCS or Joint Singles (JS) Scheme, with average gross monthly income of more than $6,000, need to pay $30,000 in additional to the flat price.
(4) First-timer households who own private property locally or overseas (including those who have disposed of one within 30 months before the date of flat application) need to pay $30,000 in additional to the flat price.
(5) First-timer Singapore citizen / Singapore permanent resident (SC/SPR) households need to pay a $10,000 premium in additional to the flat price. An SC/SPR household is one where the applicant is an SC and the other family members listed in the flat application are SPRs.
(6) All additional amounts and premiums will be adjusted according to the lease chosen.
Two-room Flexi Flats on 15- to 45-Year Leases (for offer only to elderly aged 55 and above)
(1) First-timer applicants under the SSC, NCS or Joint Singles (JS) Scheme, with average gross monthly income of more than $6,000, need to pay $30,000 in additional to the flat price.
(2) First-timer households who own private property locally or overseas (including those who have disposed of one within 30 months before the date of flat application) need to pay $30,000 in additional to the flat price.
(3) First-timer Singapore citizen / Singapore permanent resident (SC/SPR) households need to pay a $10,000 premium in additional to the flat price. An SC/SPR household is one where the applicant is an SC and the other family members listed in the flat application are SPRs.
(4) All additional amounts and premiums will be adjusted according to the lease chosen.
